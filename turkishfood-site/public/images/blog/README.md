## Image Guidelines

- **Format:** JPG or WebP preferred
- **Size:** 1200x800px minimum (will be cropped to fit)
- **Quality:** 80-85% compression
- **Style:** Authentic, appetizing, well-lit food photography

- Unsplash: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/turkish-food
